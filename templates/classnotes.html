Class Notes - Baby Fish Swim School

Swimming Basics

  • Fin flapping frequency: 3 flaps/sec
  • Turn radius: 30° tail flick
  • Bubble breathing duration: 10 mins

Water Navigation

  • Sense direction using coral positions
  • Avoid jellyfish storms
  • Follow algae trail maps

Swim Safety

  • Always swim with a buddy
  • Avoid warm currents
  • No swimming in polluted bubbles
